Interactive Story

Story Synopsis

You start your first day at Dunder Mifflin Scranton. You control how
your day will go as you interact with certain characters and follow
through certain actions. Your day at Scranton might not be quite so
enjoyable afterall.

Concepts Used

The concept that was the most impactful in our story was the while loops.
The usage of the while loop allowed us to set up certain conditions that
would influence the ending of our story. While the while loops were the
most impactful, the code that sets our story apart from other groups' is
the usage of variables. Throughout the story, we set up variables that
would be changed as you progress through the story. These variables
would later be used in the while loops in order to determine the ending
that the audience would receive.
